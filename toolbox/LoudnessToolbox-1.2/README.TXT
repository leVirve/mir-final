Loudness Toolbox:
-----------------

The LOUDNESS TOOLBOX has been downloaded from the website of GENESIS S.A.
Loudness OnLine: http://www.genesis.fr

This toolbox provides codes allowing to compute the loudness of various types of sounds, including steady-state, time-varying and impulsive sounds.
